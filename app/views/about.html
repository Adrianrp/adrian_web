Bio

I was born in Mexico City, and did most of my education in Mexico. I attended the National Autonomous University of Mexico and got a bachelor´s degree in Visual Arts. During that time, I also did one year of education abroad in the University of California/ Davis in USA. In Davis I was exhibiting in several galleries among them John Natsoulas and Phantom Gallery in Sacramento.

After finishing my education I moved to Montreal Canada for a year, and worked on mural painting for most of my staying there. I worked on several art projects among them was to make an engraving for the yearly award of RIDEAU (Réseau indépendant des diffuseurs d’événements artistiques).

A year after my life changed dramatically when I met my danish wife and decided to move to Denmark, where I live untill now together with my two sons.

"Værkerne knytter alvorlige kommentarer til menneskers og dyrs levevilkår i den moderne verden og de dilemmaer vi står overfor med hensyn til masseproduktion og genmanipulation."
